Brocolli and Beef

Description

This dish has broccoli, and beef and is cooked in a soy-ginger sauce. It can be served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 10.5 oz beef broth
  • 2 tbs white sugar
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 lb boneless round steak, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 ginger root, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the flour, beef broth, sugar and soy sauce. Stir until the sugar and flour are dissolved.
  2. In a large skillet, on high heat, cook and stir beef for 2 to 4 minutes, or until browned. Stir in broth mixture, ginger, garlic, and broccoli. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Simmer 5 to 10 minutes, or until sauce thickens.
