Crab Rangoon

Description

If you're craving for the creamy, seafoody treat of crab rangoon, here's the recipe you're looking for! This dish is served in Asian American restaurants and can be baked or fried

Ingredients

  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 6 oz canned crabmeat, dried and flaked
  • 2 thinly sliced green onions
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp light soy sauce
  • 48 wonton wrappers

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Lightly spray a baking sheet with cookiing spray.
  2. Combine the gralic, cream cheese, crab, green onions, Worchestershire sauce, and soy sauce in a bowl.
  3. Place 1 teaspoon of filling on the center of each wonton skin. Moisten the edges with water and fold the skin diagonally to form a triangle. Press the edges together. Pull both bottom corners together and seal to create a crown. Arrange the rangoon on a baking sheet and lightly spray with cooking spray.
  4. Bake in oven until golden brown, about 12 to 15 minutes.
