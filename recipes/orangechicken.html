Orange Chicken

Description

This sweet and tangy dish made in Chinese restaurants can be made at home with this recipe.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2.5 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs orange juice
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tbs green onion, chopped
  • 1 tbs orange zest, grated
  • 1/2 tsp minced fresh ginger root
  • 1/4 tsp pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 3 tbs cornstarch
  • 2 tbs water

Chicken

  • 2 large skinless, boneless, chicken breasts, cut in 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 3 ttbs olive oil

Instructions

  1. Combine the water, rice vinegar, lemon juice, soy sauce, and orange juice for sauce into a saucepan over medium-high heat. Stir in the brown sugar, green onion, orange zest, garlic, ginger, and pepper flakes. Bring to a boil. Remove from heat and cool for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Place chicken in a resealable plastic bag. Pour in 1 cup of the cooled sauce. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Reserve the remaining sauce.
  3. Mix flour, salt, and pepper in another resealable bag.
  4. Remove chicken from the sauce and place in the bag with the flour mixture. Seal the bag and shake to coat.
  5. Pour the rest of the sauce in a skillet and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  6. Mix the cornstarch and water together until completely smooth. Stir into the boiling sauce. Reduce the heat to medium-low, add the chicken, and simmer, stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes, or until cooked through.
